# Link Building Strategy (Execution Plan)

## Goal

Build high-quality backlinks that improve rankings for resume analysis, ATS score checker, and recruiter screening queries.

## Target Pages

- https://www.webresume.tech/
- https://www.webresume.tech/overview

## Anchor Text Mix

- 50% branded: WebResume.tech, WebResume ATS analyzer
- 30% partial match: AI resume analyzer, ATS score checker
- 20% natural/generic: this resume optimization tool, this platform

## 30-Day Sprint Plan

### Week 1

- Publish 2 long-form assets on your site (resume ATS checklist, interview conversion guide).
- Submit business profile and startup directory listings.
- Build a list of 100 outreach prospects (career blogs, HR newsletters, resume coaches).

### Week 2

- Send guest post pitches to top 40 prospects.
- Offer data-backed snippets from your product insights (ATS mismatch trends, keyword gap patterns).
- Request inclusion in 10 tool roundups.

### Week 3

- Publish 2 guest posts with contextual links to target pages.
- Launch a co-marketing post with a recruiting or career brand.
- Reach out to communities (Reddit/LinkedIn groups) with educational, non-spam resources.

### Week 4

- Re-contact non-responders with updated pitch angles.
- Convert unlinked brand mentions into links.
- Review acquired links and disavow obvious spam if needed.

## Quality Rules

- Prioritize relevant sites with real traffic and editorial standards.
- Avoid link farms, PBNs, and paid spam placements.
- Prefer contextual links within content over footer/sidebar links.
- Track every acquisition in a spreadsheet with URL, anchor, and status.

## KPIs

- 15-25 new referring domains in 30 days
- 5+ links to homepage and 5+ links to overview page
- Increased impressions and average position in Google Search Console
